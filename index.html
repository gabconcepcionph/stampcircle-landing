How StampCircle Works

For Business Owners

Give stamps and reward customers in under a minute—no hassle, just results.

Simple 3-Step Process:

  1. Tap the center button (QR icon) on your app's bottom navigation.
  2. Choose your action:
    • Give a Stamp - Reward customers for their purchase.
    • Reward Redemption - Let customers redeem their earned rewards.
  3. Scan the customer's QR code – they'll show their QR from the StampCircle app.

That's it! Transaction complete.

For Customers

Earning Stamps

  1. Open your StampCircle app.
  2. Show your QR code to the merchant.
  3. Get your stamp instantly.

Redeeming Rewards

  1. Select the reward you want to redeem in the app.
  2. Show your reward QR code to the merchant.
  3. Enjoy your reward!

Frequently Asked Questions

How does StampCircle work?
StampCircle replaces traditional punch cards with a digital system. Merchants give stamps to customers through the app after each purchase. Customers collect stamps and redeem them for rewards you create. It's simple, trackable, and never gets lost.
Is it suitable for my small business?
Yes! StampCircle is designed specifically for small businesses like coffee shops, restaurants, retail stores, co-working spaces, salons, and any business that wants to reward repeat customers. If you currently use punch cards or want to start a loyalty program, StampCircle is perfect for you.
Do my customers need to download an app?
Yes, customers access their stamps through the StampCircle customer portal. It's a simple web-based platform that works on any smartphone, so no complicated app store downloads are required.
Can I customize my rewards?
Absolutely! You have complete control over your rewards. Set how many stamps are needed, what rewards customers can redeem, and create special promotions or limited-time offers. Make it uniquely yours.
How do I give stamps to customers?
It's super easy! From your merchant portal, simply scan the customer's QR code or enter their ID, and tap to give a stamp. The whole process takes just seconds at checkout.
Can I track my program's performance?
Yes! The merchant portal includes analytics showing active users, stamp activity, popular rewards, and transaction history. You'll have insights to help optimize your loyalty program.
What if I have multiple staff members?
No problem! You can add multiple staff members to your merchant account. Each staff member can give stamps and process redemptions, making it easy to manage your loyalty program across your team.
Can I run special promotions or events?
Yes! StampCircle includes features for creating special events, time-limited promotions, and giveaways. Perfect for holidays, anniversaries, or driving traffic during slow periods.
